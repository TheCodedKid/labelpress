# Privacy Policy — LabelPress

**Last updated:** February 28, 2026

## What LabelPress Does

LabelPress is a Google Sheets add-on that generates printable Avery label PDFs from your spreadsheet data.

## Data Access

LabelPress requests the following permissions:

- **Read your spreadsheet data** — to pull the column headers and row data you select for your labels
- **Create Google Docs** — to generate a formatted label document used to produce the PDF
- **Create files in Google Drive** — to save the generated PDF to your Drive

## Data Storage & Sharing

- **No data is stored.** LabelPress does not collect, store, or retain any of your spreadsheet data, personal information, or generated documents.
- **No data is shared.** Your data is never transmitted to any external server, third party, or analytics service.
- **No tracking.** LabelPress does not use cookies, analytics, or any form of usage tracking.

## How Your Data Is Used

All processing happens entirely within your Google account using Google Apps Script. Your spreadsheet data is read in real time to generate labels, and the output (a Google Doc and PDF) is saved directly to your own Google Drive. Nothing leaves your Google environment.

## Data Retention

LabelPress does not retain any data. The generated Google Doc and PDF remain in your Drive under your control — you can delete them at any time.

## Third-Party Services

LabelPress does not use any third-party services, APIs, or external servers. It runs entirely on Google's Apps Script platform within your Google account.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, changes will be posted to this page with an updated date.
